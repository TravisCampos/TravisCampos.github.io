Crypto Visual

Crypto Visual is a Mobile Application that allows users to track and monitor digital assets, providing access to information on Crytpo, Exchanges, Markets, and News. The main purpose of Crypto Visual is to enable users to create a Watchlist of cryptocurrencies that are updated in real-time, allowing users to view the intraday price of any cryptocurrency covered by the Mobile Application. Crypto Visual can also be checked regularly for real-time quotes on Foreign Exchange Markets and Commodity Markets, including access to real-time values of Currency Pairs along with real-time prices of Precious Metals like Gold and Silver. Crypto Visual also provides users access to daily volume data about Cryptocurrency Exchanges. The BTC Volume of many Cryptocurreny Exchanges is compared in order to develop a ranking system that is updated based on the volume of BTC traded. As a final feature, Crypto Visual allows users access to News and Research articles that often examine and explain the major events and developments in the world of cryptocurrency. The News Articles are updated regularly and usually analyze the macro-economic and micro-economic factors of the global economy that affect the world of digital assets.
